I teach Artificial Intelligence (BCS-13), Machine Learning (BCS-27) for B.Tech students, and advanced courses like Internet of Things and Computer Vision & Image Processing for M.Tech students at MMMUT Gorakhpur.

Yes, I am always looking for motivated M.Tech students with strong backgrounds in programming and a genuine passion for AI and IoT research. I have successfully supervised 12+ M.Tech dissertations. Please apply through MMMUT's official admission process and feel free to email me with your research interests.

My research spans Artificial Intelligence, Face Recognition, and Internet of Things (IoT). I'm particularly interested in developing intelligent systems for security, automation, and real-world applications combining AI with IoT technologies.

I'm open to research collaborations with both academic and industry partners. If you have an interesting research problem or project idea in AI, Face Recognition, or IoT, please send me an email at dsscse@mmmut.ac.in with a brief description of your proposal.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science & Engineering and completed my Post Doctoral Fellowship from Malaysia. I am a member of several international professional bodies including Computer Society of India (CSI), Internet Society (ISOC) USA, AAAI USA, and IAENG Hong Kong.
